## Subquery factoring = with clause
```sql
with a as 
(select 
deptno, 
avg(sal) as avg_sal from emp group by deptno)
select e.empno, e.sal, a.deptno, 
to_char(a.avg_sal, '9999.99') as avg_sal
from emp e, a
where e.deptno = a.deptno
and e.sal > a.avg_sal
order by 1;
```
